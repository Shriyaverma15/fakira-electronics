About Us - Fakira Electronics

Who We Are?

Welcome to Fakira Electronics, your one-stop shop for the latest in cutting-edge technology! Founded in the heart of Gandhi Chowk, Hamirpur, we are a team of tech enthusiasts committed to offering you high-quality electronic products at unbeatable prices.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To provide our customers with top-tier electronic products and unmatched customer service. We believe in delivering value through high-quality, durable products at affordable prices, making the latest tech accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading electronics retailer in Hamirpur, known for our exceptional products, competitive prices, and outstanding customer service.

What We Do

At Fakira Electronics, we offer a diverse selection of electronic gadgets, including:

  • Smartphones: The latest smartphones from top brands, including Apple, Samsung, Xiaomi, and more.
  • Smartwatches: Stylish and functional smartwatches that keep you connected on the go.
  • Wristwatches: Classic and modern wristwatches for every occasion.
  • Wall Clocks: Unique and stylish wall clocks to add a touch of elegance to your home or office.
  • Accessories: A wide range of accessories, including phone cases, chargers, headphones, speakers and more.

Our Values

  • Quality First: We handpick every product we sell to ensure it meets the highest standards of quality.
  • Customer-Centric: We are dedicated to providing excellent customer service and ensuring that your experience with us is seamless from start to finish.
  • Innovation: We stay ahead of the curve by offering the latest tech and electronics, ensuring that our customers always have access to the newest gadgets and accessories.
  • Affordability: Our goal is to make cutting-edge technology affordable for everyone.

Our Journey

Founded in 2000, Fakira Electronics began as a small local shop with a big dream: bringing the best technology to Hamirpur. Over time, we've grown into a trusted name in electronics, offering quality products to thousands of customers.
