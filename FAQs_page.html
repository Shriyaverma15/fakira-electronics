Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Questions

What products do you offer at Fakira Electronics?

We offer a wide range of electronics including smartphones, wristwatches, wall clocks, tablets, accessories, headphones, earphones and audio equipment. All our products are sourced from trusted brands to ensure quality and durability.

How can I place an order on your website?

Although you cannot place an order directly through the website, you can contact us via WhatsApp, Instagram, or other communication channels to inform us of your desired product. We will reserve it for you, and you can visit our store in person to complete the billing and purchasing process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, online payment options such as PhonePe and Paytm, and most importantly, cash payments as well.

Do you offer any kind of shipping or home delivery?

Apologies, but we are not offering delivery services at the moment. However, we may consider introducing this option in the future.

Do your products come with a warranty?

Yes, we do offer warranties and guarantees on products, which vary depending on the product and brand. The duration of the warranty may range from 6 months to 2 years, as it is determined by the specific product and its brand.
